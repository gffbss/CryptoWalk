Crypt(o) Walk

 
 
 
 
 
 

Crypt(o) Walk is a place that was built to service the needs of those interested in learning more about cryptocurrencies. Cryptocurrencies are peer-to-peer Internet currencies that enable instant payments to anyone in the world, with no middle man (ie a Bank). Cryptocurrnecies are usually obtained through mining, but thanks to the community there have been many creations, like marketplaces, that allow the average internet user to get their hands on an Internet currency by simply making a purchase online.

For those looking to get started there is one very important first step. You must get a wallet! Wallets are exaclty what they sound like, but in computer form. There are two types of wallets. Desktop wallets that come as downloadable programs to install on your machine, or online wallets hosted and secured by the wallet provider. Each coin below will have links to set one up.

Currently we feature three of the most prevalant, promising cryptocurrencies, Bitcoin, Dogecoin and Litecoin. The current buy price shown for each is a reflection of 1 USD to 1 of the selected currency.
